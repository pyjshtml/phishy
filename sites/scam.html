What is Phishing?

Phishing is the fraudulent attempt to obtain sensitive information or data, such as usernames, passwords and credit card details or other sensitive details, by impersonating oneself as a trustworthy entity in a digital communication. Phishing often directs users to enter personal information at a fake website which matches the look and feel of the legitimate site. For example, this website emulates Facebook's user interface.

padding

How can I recognize a phishing attempt?

No legitimate bank or business would send you an email requesting that you re-enter your personal information. Mispellings, poor grammar, and typos are all indicators of a phishing email. If you receive a phishing email, the best thing to do is to just ignore it and mark it as spam.
